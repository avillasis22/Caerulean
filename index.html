About Caerulean

The Caerulean NFT is a collection of Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) that will raise money for SCH Academy. These unique pieces of digital art are inspired by the very first Chestnut Hill Academy yearbook - Caerulean 1911. In this yearbook, an artist named W. G. Temple, the class valedictorian drew original etchings for each of the 24 graduates.

My work involved turning these sketches into digital assets and then animating them. It’s a tribute to the artist, the first Chestnut Hill Academy graduating class, and our community as a whole. The first NFT for bid is Edward W. Kling, a 1911 graduate who attended Harvard University.

Next Steps

1. Mint the first animated NFT Edward W. Kling via a live auction that is closing May 14th
